How was filming "Black Hawk down"

As for the filming process, the film "Black Hawk Down" was filmed mainly in Morocco, as shooting in Somalia was not possible due to political instability in that country. Real military helicopters and equipment were used to create combat scenes. Director Ridley Scott sought to create the most realistic perception of the events that took place in Somalia in 1993.
