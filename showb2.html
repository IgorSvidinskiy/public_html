The matrix

Year: 1999 Rating: 8.7 Director: Vachovskiy brothers

Description

The film opens with Trinity and Cypher having a phone conversation with green numbers running across the screen. Trinity notices that their conversation is being tapped by someone, after which she deftly fights off the police who came after her and runs away from the Agents. At the last moment of the chase, she reaches the ringing phone in a phone booth and picks up the phone. The agent driving the truck crashes into the booth, slamming it into the wall. However, Trinity's body is no longer there. Young man Thomas Anderson leads a double life. During the day he is a programmer in a large company, and at night he is a hacker Neo. One day, a strange message arrives on his personal computer: “You are stuck in the Matrix” (The Matrix has you). An unknown person instructs him to "follow the white rabbit".
